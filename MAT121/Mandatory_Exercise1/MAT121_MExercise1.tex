Solving $A\Vec{x}=\Vec{b}$. We look at the reduced echelon form of $B$ and that gives us this system:
\begin{flalign*}
x_{1} & & + 2x_{4} &= 3 &\\
    &x_{2} & &= 1\\
      & &x_{3} + x_{4} &= 2 &\\
\end{flalign*}

Gives us solution:
\begin{flalign*}
x_{1} &= 3-2x_{4} &\\
x_{2} &= 1 &\\
x_{3} &= 2-x_{4} &\\
x_{4} &= free
\end{flalign*}
